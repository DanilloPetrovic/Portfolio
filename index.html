About me

Hello, my name is Danilo Petrović and I am 16 years old. I live in Novi Pazar and attend a course in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. I go to a technical school in Novi Pazar and my interests are sports and programming.

Sports provide me with an opportunity to relax and clear my mind, while programming presents a challenge for my imagination and creativity. Combining both hobbies allows me to constantly grow and progress.

By attending the HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React course, I hope to gain as much knowledge and experience as possible in the field of web programming. I believe that this education will help me achieve my goals and dreams.

I am always ready to learn something new and develop my skills, and I am motivated to create something useful and creative in the world of technology.

In short, I am Danilo Petrović, a sports enthusiast and a programmer, always ready for new challenges and the development of my knowledge and skills.
